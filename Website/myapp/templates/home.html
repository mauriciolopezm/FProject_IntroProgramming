HOW IS VIOLENCE RELATED TO SUBSTANCE ABUSE?

The homicide rate in many US City is increasing year after year. In Chicago the streets are especially bloody. Many scholars have been researching the causes for the high rates in violence in the past. Correlation between variables such as weather and homicide rates are well known. However, policy makers are searching for research that reveals independent variables, which can be directly influenced by policies. Hence the relation between substance abuse and violence could reveal important insights from which policy implications could be derived. The substance of Marijuana is frequently consumed in Chicago. It is demonstrated to have modifying effects on the brain structure. Therefore consumption of Marijuana is likely to act as a mediating variable have an effect on crimes through the effect the consumption has on the brain. In order to control for weather (it could also influence the amount of Marijuana consumed due to production), we use CHICAGO and SAN FRANCISCO for our research.
